Flexbox Shorthand!

  • Keywork values

    flex:auto

    flex:initial

    flex:none

  • One value, unit-less number: flex-grow

    flex:2;

  • One value, width/height flex-basis(width)

    flex:10em;

    flex:30%;

    flex:min-content;

  • Two values, flex-grow | flex-shrink

    flex:2 2

  • Three values: flex-grow | flex-shrink | flex-basis

    flex:2 2 10%

  • Global vlaues

    flex:inherit

    flex:inital

    flex:unset
