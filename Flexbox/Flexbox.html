Within one container how do we distribute space across different elements. Make those elements larger or add space between those elements, move them to the left or right, split up the space evenly, Make sure the first thing gets more space middle thing gets least amout of space, we can create flexible layouts ( the elements inside are flexible) as the browser is resized the boxes are flexing or conforming to a desired shape or position.
